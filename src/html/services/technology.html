Drupal

Come for the software, stay for the community.

Collaborate

Drupal's focus on social publishing can help you create sites and applications to help your users express their opinions and engage with one another. You can have tight control over who can create, view, administer, publish and otherwise interact with content on your site


Extend for miles.

With more than 16,000 available modules, the vast majority of your site's requirements can be addressed with Drupal core and available add-on modules. Focus your efforts on building great web sites, not re-creating features available elsewhere. You can even contribute modules for others to use.

WxT

The Web Experience Toolkit

Mobile First Responsive

WxT uses a mobile first approach using Panopoly and AdaptiveTheme, adapts to different screen sizes and device capabilities, has touchscreen support, is optimized for performance and is building support for device-based mobile applications. Why wouldn't you use WxT in government sites ?


Reusable, Accessible & Multilingual

WxT uses ready-made, reusable tools and components for building and maintaining innovative websites that are accessible, usable and interoperable. WxT complies with Web Accessibility and Web Usability standards and makes use of new technologies such as CSS3, HTML5 and jquery.

With ever growing support for widgets and plugins, Drupal WxT is great for both internal and external websites. With WxT supporting multilingual sites requiring WCAG 2.0 Level AA, its no wonder why it is recommended for Government of Canada websites.

Solr

Blazing fast open source search.

Performance Improvements

High traffic sites running search queries against the database can start to degrade the site’s overall performance if the database becomes the bottleneck. This is also true for lower traffic sites with lots of content. Complex search queries can be slow to run. Drupals Solr search helps with these performance issues and speeds up your site.


Apache Solr features

Its major features include powerful full-text search, hit highlighting, faceted search, near real-time indexing, dynamic clustering, database integration, rich document (e.g., Word, PDF) handling, and geospatial search.


Scalable & Reliable

Solr is highly reliable, scalable and fault tolerant, providing distributed indexing, replication and load-balanced querying, automated failover and recovery, centralized configuration and more. Solr powers the search and navigation features of many of the world's largest internet sites.
