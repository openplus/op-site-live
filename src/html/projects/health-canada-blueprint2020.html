Health Canada - Blueprint 2020

Health Canada is the Federal department responsible for helping Canadians maintain and improve their health, while respecting individual choices and circumstances.

Health Canada took part in the Blueprint 2020 initiative, a Government of Canada's national initiative to promote a modern workplace that makes smart use of new technologies to improve networking, access to data and customer service.

The Challenge

OpenPlus was asked to design an interactive and engaging solution that will stand out during the Blueprint 2020 event. After the event, the same solution will be integrated into Health Canada’s intranet for their 50,000 employees.

The solution had to load digital content in English and French, work online as well as offline, standalone as well as part of Health Canada’s Intranet.

Requirements

Work on touch devices (TV, kiosk, mobile)

Work online and offline

Work as a standalone app

Integrate with Health Canada's intranet

Accessible, Multilingual and Responsive

The Solution

OpenPlus has designed, prototyped, and developed an interactive dashboard that works across multiple devices including Health Canada’s Intranet.

We used HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript to create a cross-platform compatible app that is responsive, multilingual and accessible.

From the same source code, PhoneGap was used to build a native app for mobile and tablets, both iOS and Android. The native app allowed us to load content for offline consumption.

Using animations and interactions, the dashboard lets employees discover digital content in a more engaging and interactive way.

Interactive Kiosk Concept
