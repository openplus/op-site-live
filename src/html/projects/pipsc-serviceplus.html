PIPSC ServicePlus

The Professional Institute of the Public Service of Canada (PIPSC) was founded in 1920. With over 57,000 members, the Institute is the largest union in Canada representing scientists and professionals employed at the federal and some provincial and territorial levels of government.

The ServicePlus is a member benefit program that represents some 80,000 federal, provincial and private sector professionals within Canada.

ServicePlus memebers can take advantage of preferential rates and savings on a variety of products and services available exclusively to members.
