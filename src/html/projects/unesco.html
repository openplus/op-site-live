UNESCO Institute for Statistics

The UNESCO Institute for Statistics (UIS) is the official and trusted source of internationally-comparable data on education, science, culture and communication. As the official statistical agency of UNESCO, the UIS produces a wide range of indicators in UNESCO's fields of action by working with national statistical offices, line ministries and other statistical organizations.

The UIS produces the data to fuel the policies and investments needed to transform lives and propel the world towards its development goals.

The UNESCO Institute for Statistics (UIS) wished to upgrade its web presence in order to deliver content that is engaging and interactive to meet today's user experience.

Client Requirements

The client required an extremely short delivery timeline, with many different working parts still to be discovered and integrated.

This presented a wonderful challenge with respect to gathering business requirements as well as overcoming technical uncertainties at the onset of the project. We were able to work with UNESCO to define many of the as-of-yet defined interfaces, recommend industry-best-practices when it comes to deploying on Azure, and collaborating with an extended team of professionals delivering many of the underlying data API that needed integration into Drupal. The final deliverable was provided on schedule and on budget, despite the high number of challenges and risks.
