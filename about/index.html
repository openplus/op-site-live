Get to Know Us

Just a few words about OpenPlus.

Ideology.

We believe Content Architecture is as important as Information Architecture, and really know how to build websites that are responsive, WCAG accessible & multilingual. From collaborative intranets to public multi-sites to custom mobile & tablet applications. We can help bring governance and help the adoption process.

Transparency.

We are transparent in everything we do. OpenPlus will give you the best service and will hide nothing. We build and deliver awesome projects and only work with select clients. Before starting any project, we always have a discovery process to find out and differentiate between you and your users needs and wants.

Experience.

With over a decade of trusted expertise, our team of professionals have unmatched experience creating content architecture that works and has platform know-how from the old to the new. We are a team of experienced digital professionals with a passion for enterprise content management solutions.
